Pike Alumni

Pi Kappa Alpha is one of the oldest fraternities in the nation, and as a result we also have one of the largest alumni networks. There are currently over 250,000 lifetime initiates and over 14,000 undergraduate members of the Fraternity. It is not uncommon for a Pike to encounter another Pike at random in public and instantly begin a conversation as if they are old friends.

Alpha Delta Alumni

Our Alpha Delta Chapter alumni have recently been making great contributions to the Alpha Delta Chapter. We have recently re-established a fully functioning Housing Corporation while also beginning the initial steps to create an alumni association specific to the Alpha Delta Chapter. Our alumni board continues to offer assistance, advice, and support in all facets of the Fraternity. With their support, we continue to thrive as a chapter. We love having our alumni over at the house, especially during football season when we can all get together and belt out a mighty "To Hell With Georgia!"
